About Us

Northeast Sound Mixer is led by Nick Natale, an experienced sound recordist and audio engineer serving New England. From film sets to live events, we deliver precision sound, professional setups, and reliable execution — every time.

With a decade of experience in sound mixing, video editing, and live production, we serve a wide range of clients including filmmakers, corporations, and event producers across Vermont, New Hampshire, Massachusetts, and Boston.
